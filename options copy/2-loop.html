Tales From The Loop


Stranger Things, The Goonies, Monster Squad. This is the RPG equivalent. (Though mostly I think it'll play like the Goonies.)

You will play a character between the ages of 10 and 15, living in an alternate universe version of the 1980s that has elements of super science.

It’s a pretty rules-lite game. (You can’t really min-max a 10 year old.) It’s also a bit different from other RPGs like Dungeons & Dragons in that players are expected to give a lot of input to their own scenes and the backstory is a big part of the game. Basically coming up with their relationships with the other kids, with their parents/teachers/other adults (who they will have scenes with).

I’d like to do a one-shot before Halloween. It should take 3-5 hours.
